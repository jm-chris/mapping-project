description: The Kangzhan wenxian shuju pingtai (抗战文献数据平台 / Data Platform of the Anti-Japanese
  War and Modern China-Japan Relations) is a comprehensive free database developed
  by the Institute of Modern History of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences (CASS).
  While primarily focused on the Sino-Japanese War (1937-1945) and Sino-Japanese relations,
  the platform provides access to a wide range of digitized Republican Period resources
  including archives, books, newspapers, journals, communist publications, audio recordings,
  photographs, and musical scores. Materials span both official and unofficial sources,
  offering diverse perspectives on this critical period in Chinese history.
