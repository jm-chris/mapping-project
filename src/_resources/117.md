description: 'The database "Foreign Office Files for China, 1919-1980" contains complete
  digitized images of all British Foreign Office files dealing with China, Hong Kong
  and Taiwan during published during the period 1919-1980. There are six subsections:
  1919-1929: Kuomintang, CCP and the Third International; 1930-1937: The Long March,
  civil war in China and the Manchurian Crisis; 1938-1948: Open Door, Japanese war
  and the seeds of Communist victory; 1949-1956: The Communist revolution; 1957-1966:
  The Great Leap Forward; and 1967-1980: The Cultural Revolution.'
