description: 'A Comprehensive Record of China''s Reform and Opening Up (中国改革开放全纪录)
  covering 1978-2018, documenting the history of reform, telling reform stories, and
  building consensus on reform. Adopting a chronological format, the database consists
  of four major parts: annual highlights, chronology of major events, oral history
  of reform, and material evidence/artifacts. Events are listed with links to full-text
  of speeches, images, articles, and oral history recordings. Full use of the website
  (including downloading of articles and images) requires setting up a personal account.'
