description: Early Chinese Periodicals Online (ECPO) is an open-access research platform
  developed by Heidelberg Research Architecture (HRA). ECPO joins together several
  important digital collections of the early Chinese press and puts them into a single
  overarching framework. So far, the ECPO project has focused on a body of rich but
  heretofore undervalued materials—women’s and entertainment magazines.. The platform
  provides comprehensive access to over 300 publications comprising approximately
  435,000 entries including 300,000 scans, 85,000 records, and 50,000 agent names
  from Republican-era magazines and newspapers. ECPO combines extensive approaches
  (comprehensive catalogs of periodicals with basic metadata) and intensive approaches
  (cover-to-cover digitization with detailed content analysis and structured metadata).
  Collections include Chinese Women's Magazines in the Late Qing and Early Republican
  Period (WoMag database - four influential magazines published 1904-1937), Chinese
  Entertainment Newspapers (Xiaobao 小報), and selections of literary and art magazines.
  The platform provides fully bilingual metadata in Chinese and English, making it
  accessible to scholars beyond Chinese studies. All bibliographic data is available
  through the ECPO API in MODS XML format, and metadata is published on heiDATA, Heidelberg
  University's research data repository.
