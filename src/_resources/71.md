description: 'Chinese Theater Guide is a teaching and learning resource for modern
  iterations of traditional Chinese theater, developed by Ohio State University. The
  site is organized around six iconic plays tracing their afterlives across theatrical,
  filmic, print, and other media forms. Featured plays represent major genres and
  themes in Chinese theatrical tradition. For each play, the site provides: an introductory
  page discussing the original play''s historical context, plot, characters, and theatrical
  conventions; separate modules for different early modern and modern productions
  showing how the play has been adapted and reinterpreted over time; texts (scripts,
  librettos), images (performance photographs, costume designs, stage sets), and video
  clips documenting specific productions.'
