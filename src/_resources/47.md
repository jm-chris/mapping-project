description: Historical Photographs of China (HPC) is a free online database hosted
  by the University of Bristol containing over 21,000 images primarily from collections
  in the UK and Europe. The project aggregates and provides access to historical photographs
  of China from numerous archives, libraries, museums, and private collections, making
  geographically dispersed materials accessible through a single searchable platform.
  The collection spans the late 19th and early-to-mid 20th centuries, and include
  urban and rural scenes, daily life, architecture, landscapes, cultural practices,
  political events, foreign settlements, and missionary activities. The platform includes
  metadata, contextual information, and links to original holding institutions.
