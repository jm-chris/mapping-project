description: The PRC History Group is a network of scholars with interests in the
  history of the People's Republic of China, defining history broadly to encompass
  various disciplinary approaches and including decades prior to 1949. The Group manages
  H-PRC (an H-Net channel) that hosts scholarly discussions and distributes news and
  announcements of interest to PRC historians. The site publishes the PRC History
  Review, which contains research articles, roundtable discussions, and reviews on
  recent scholarship in PRC history. Other resources include Chinese-language journals
  focusing on topics like the Cultural Revolution (e.g., Remembrance 记忆, Yesterday
  昨天, Past Events 往事) and links to freely available resources on PRC History. The
  Group welcomes scholars, students, researchers, and others with serious interest
  in PRC History.
