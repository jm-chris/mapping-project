description: Taiwan eBook (臺灣華文電子書庫) is a digital preservation and access initiative
  created and maintained by the National Central Library (NCL) of Taiwan. The database
  digitizes Chinese language books published between 1911-1949 (the Republican period)
  held by libraries, research organizations, and government institutions throughout
  Taiwan. These works document various aspects of Republican-era history, culture,
  literati, geography, art, literature, society, politics, and economy.
