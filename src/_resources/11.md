description: 'The Hong Kong Literature Database (香港文學資料庫) is the first comprehensive
  database on Hong Kong literature, developed by the Chinese University of Hong Kong
  Library System. As of June 2024, the database contains nearly 670,000 records. Collection
  includes: over 200 Hong Kong literary journals, literary supplements from 20 Hong
  Kong early newspapers, approximately 18,000 monographs, with over 180,000 items
  available as full-text images. The database provides multiple search functions including
  full-text search and image viewing, accessible without geographic restrictions.
  It serves as an online platform providing literary news, literary organization activities,
  and writer websites, supporting teaching and research on Hong Kong literature.'
