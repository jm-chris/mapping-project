description: 'Wanfang’s Dissertations and theses database. This database includes
  more than 2 million dissertations and theses, and hence is much larger than the
  history and literature (doctoral) dissertations and (master) theses parts of the
  CNKI equivalent previously available. Note that you can narrow down your results
  not only by using the topical and chronological facets, but also by typing within
  the tab-looking grey boxes on top of your results: e.g., by typing 北京大学 in the 学校
  box. As with all Wanfang databases, once on the Wanfang home page, you can move
  from database to database via tabs. The “xue shu lun wen” tab combines several databases
  into one mega-search, and you can also combine searches across databases using the
  advanced search option.'
