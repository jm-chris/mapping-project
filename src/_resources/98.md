description: The TEPS (Taiwan Electronic Periodicals Service) database now included
  in the Airiti Digital Library offers an archive of several hundred Taiwanese periodicals
  in various subjects, including natural and applied sciences, medicine, the humanities,
  and social sciences. Full texts can be downloaded in PDF format. One can browse
  by title. The TEPS collection is part of the Chinese Electronic Periodical Service
  (CEPS) database. The majority of the journals included in the database are covered
  in electronic form from 2000 on-wards, but there are many journals with digital
  back issues going back to the mid-20th century. The database even includes some
  1930s journals.
