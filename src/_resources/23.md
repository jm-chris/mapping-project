description: 'The online system of the Archives of the Institute of Modern History
  (AIMH), Academia Sinica, provides readers with catalog search and digital image
  viewing. The documents can be roughly grouped into five categories: Foreign Ministry
  Collection (1861-2009), Economic Ministry Collection (1903-1980), Personal Papers,
  Other Organizations, and Historical Maps. The total length of all files combined
  is approximately 10,000 meters. The Foreign Ministry Collection includes records
  from the Office in Charge of Foreign Affairs/Zongli yamen (1861-1900), the Ministry
  of External Affairs (1901-1911), the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Northern
  Government (1912-1928), and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of China
  (1928-2009). The Economic Ministry Collection, the largest single set of materials,
  includes records from the Commerce Ministry (1903-1906), the Ministry of Agriculture
  and Commerce (1913-1927), the Ministry of Industry (1930-1938), the Ministry of
  Economic Affairs (1938-1980), and various economic offices after the Nationalist
  Government retreated to Taiwan. Half of the holdings have been digitized; 60 percent
  of digitized items can be viewed online, while the rest require on-site reading
  room access.'
