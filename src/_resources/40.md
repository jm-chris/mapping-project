description: DocuSky (數位人文學術研究平台 / Digital Humanities Scholarly Research Platform)
  is a comprehensive online collaboration platform created by the Digital Humanities
  Research Center (DHRC) at National Taiwan University. The platform serves as an
  integrated environment hosting datasets, digital tools, and learning resources specifically
  designed for digital humanities research. DocuSky provides researchers with tools
  for text analysis, data visualization, corpus building, and collaborative research
  projects. The platform supports multiple Chinese text analysis functions including
  full-text search, concordance, statistical analysis, and network visualization.
  Researchers can upload their own corpora, use shared datasets, and apply various
  analytical tools without requiring advanced programming skills. DocuSky has been
  widely adopted by digital humanities researchers in Taiwan and internationally for
  projects in classical Chinese literature, historical documents, religious texts,
  and linguistic studies.
